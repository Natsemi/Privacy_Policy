Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Natsemi collects, uses, and protects your information while using our Discord bot and related services. By using Natsemi, you consent to the practices outlined here.

1. Data We Collect

  • Server data: server name, ID, member list, invite links
  • User data: user ID, username, join/leave actions
  • Moderation actions: ban, kick, bulk message deletion
  • Public profile/contact info: when you use introduction or bot info commands

2. Use of Data

  • Data is used to operate features, enhance service functionality, and support moderation and welcoming/goodbye messaging.
  • No personal or sensitive data is sold or shared with third parties except for legal, security, or support purposes.

3. Data Protection

  • Natsemi is designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy regulations.
  • All data is stored securely and only as necessary for bot function.
  • You may request deletion, correction, or inspection of your data by contacting code.natsemi.rui@gmail.com.

4. Children's Privacy

  • Natsemi is not intended for children under 13. No data from underage users is knowingly collected.

5. Policy Changes

  • This Privacy Policy may be revised at any time. Updates will be posted via Discord channels or our official website.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, data requests, or legal issues, please email: code.natsemi.rui@gmail.com
